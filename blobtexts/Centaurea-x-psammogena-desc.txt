<strong>General Description: </strong>
Hybrid Knapweed is a member of the Asteraceae (Sunflower) 
Family and is a hybrid of Spotted and Diffuse knapweeds.
Traits are variable and will share traits from both Spotted and Diffuse Knapweeds.
